Modern Tech

In today's world, computers have reached astonishing levels of power and efficiency. We've not only developed incredibly potent mainframe computers but also crafted remarkably small yet capable single board computers (SBCs). These SBCs, like the Raspberry Pi 5 and Raspberry Pi Pico, showcase the remarkable strides we've made in technology. They pack a punch far surpassing that of the first computers that guided humanity to the moon. It's truly awe-inspiring to witness how much computing power we now hold in the palm of our hands.
